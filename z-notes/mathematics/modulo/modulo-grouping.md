## Modulo Grouping

### Trigger
Indices or Indices relation are linked by modulo x, like j-i % x, or j % x, etc.

### Key Idea
- There will be exactly x groups
- Split by relation and follow constraint per group

### One Liner
There will be x groups for modulo x

### Mistake
- Treating as a single problem instead of independent grouping
