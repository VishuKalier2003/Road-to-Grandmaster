## Greedy Digits

### Trigger
Digit replacement + constraint on digit sum

### Key Idea
- sum is commutative (a+b)=(b+a) and since order does not matter, not DP but greedy
- filling constraints by either largest, smallest or any other ranking

### One Liner
- finding digits of number + greedy approach on list of digits
