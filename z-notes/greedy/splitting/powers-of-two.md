## Powers of two

### Trigger
Splitting powers of two to get min, max

### Key Idea
- The value 2^n is larger even than the sum of (2^1, ... 2^n-1)

### One Liner
- Treat 2^n specially since the weight is exponential not linear

### Mistake
- Thought about splitting into first and second half
