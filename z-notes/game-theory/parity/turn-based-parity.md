## Turn Based Parity

### Trigger
The amount or resource decreases or increases at each turn by a fixed value, and the winner is decided by the initial value of the resource.

### Key Idea
- Reduce simulation to an cumulative identity like sum, product, series, etc.

### One Liner
- the winner is predetermined from the inital state itself
