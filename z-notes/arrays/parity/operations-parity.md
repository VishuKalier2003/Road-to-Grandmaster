## Operations Parity

### Trigger
Multiple type of operations (sum, product, etc.) and satisfying parity

### Key Idea
- Find the larger overlapping operation that satisfies parity
- Greedy more from larger overlap to inner operation

### One Liner
- find operation that triggers other operations, begin from top greedily

### Mistake
- Couldn't infer the type of operation to perform first
