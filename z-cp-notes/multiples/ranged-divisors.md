## Ranged Multiples

### Trigger
When
- problem asks to check a divisibility of a number across all the numbers in a range

### Mistakes
- Using division normally

### Idea
- Find lcm of the entire range and then check the feasibility of solution
- The solution will be feasible if number x is divisible by lcm

### One Liner
- Evaluate lcm and reach the feasible solution
